About FNATIC Valorant Sports Team

FNATIC are one of the longest serving organizations in professional esports, having been founded in 2004 in London, England. Their VALORANT division was established in February 2021 aftering signing SUMN FC. Fnatic are notoriously known for their dominance in Counter-Strike throughout their tenure, they have also picked up mutliple titled in League of Legends. Fnatic also compete in Dota 2, Halo, Rainbow Six: Seige, Apex Legends and FIFA.

FNATIC's Valorant team is admired for their teamwork, innovative strategies, and individual skill. They often engaged in exciting matches against other established esports organizations, making their games a highlight of the Valorant competitive scene.
